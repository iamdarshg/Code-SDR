WIDEBAND SDR PROJECT STRUCTURE
============================

Complete software package structure for the 1 MHz - 10 GHz Wideband SDR

Performance Specifications:
===========================
- Frequency Range: 1 MHz - 10 GHz
- Sample Rate: 1 kSPS - 105 MSPS
- ADC Resolution: 10 bits
- USB Throughput: 280-320 Mbps
- Latency: <15 ms ADC to application
- Noise Figure: 6-8 dB (with LNA)
- Frequency Accuracy: <1 ppm
